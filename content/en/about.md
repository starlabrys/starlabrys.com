# About Starlabrys

Starlabrys combines **Star** with **Labrys** — the labrys is the double-bitted axe of ancient Greek/Minoan civilization, a symbol of decisive power.

It echoes the company's Chinese name, "星钺" (Xīng Yuè): **Star** for guidance and ideals, **Yuè** (axe) for boldness and decisiveness. That balance of vision and resolve is how we approach hard technical problems.
